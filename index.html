UIT-RGPV, Bhopal is a community of teachers, staff & students, who are dedicated to the achievement of a shared vision of excellence. The community believes in renewing and transferring knowledge & skills and the development of positive & purposeful attitudes and values. Being in the campus of the State Technological University, the UIT-RGPV is in a position to offer excellent and high quality academic services to the students. The Institution is situated in Bhopal, a city well known for its natural beauty & lakes. The city is well connected to the other parts of the country, by air, rail, and road. The large number of industries situated in and around Bhopal, provide excellent training opportunities to the students and staff.
